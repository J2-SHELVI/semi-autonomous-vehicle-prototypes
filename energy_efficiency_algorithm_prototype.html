Energy Efficiency Algorithm Prototype for Semi-Autonomous Electric Vehicles

This prototype focuses on the adaptive cruise control aspect of energy efficiency.

Algorithm

Here is a simplified representation of the algorithm:


1. Initialize variables
2. While driving:
3.  Determine the velocity of the leading vehicle
4.  Adjust the following vehicle's speed to maintain a safe distance
5.  If safe distance is achieved, reduce power consumption
6.  If power consumption is below a threshold, switch to energy-saving mode
7.  If energy-saving mode, prioritize battery regeneration during deceleration
8.  Log power consumption, velocity, and distance data
9. End While
10. Analyze logged data to evaluate the algorithm's effectiveness
